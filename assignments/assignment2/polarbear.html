The Polar Bear

Overview

The Polar Bear primarily lives inside the Arctic Circle. It is a large bear, probabaly the largest of the 3 kinds of bears They spend most of their time on sea ice, looking for prey underneath the ice such as seals and fish. When there is limited food, Polar bears will survive off of their body fat for several weeks at a time. Polar Bears are the most aggressive out of the 3 major bear types. They tend to be stealthy when hunting their prey, and the victim is often unaware that the bear is there until they are about to be eaten.
